What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground
Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and has fired stove.
The lucury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hold and cold running water.
Shower and restroom facilities are also located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax!
Most guest also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather
with layers of clothing.
